What is Mailspatch?

Mailspatch is a test mail server solution that allows testing email notifications without sending them to the real users of your application. Not only does Mailspatch work as a powerful email test tool, it also lets you view your dummy emails online, forward them to your regular mailbox, share with the team and more! Mailspatch is a mail server test tool built by Railsware Product Studio LLC, a premium software development consulting company.
